Project Idea

 

 

Overview

 

An IT Project that our group came up with together is a food delivery app that is paired with a delivery robot, this app will be available on both the Appstore and  The food delivery app itself is a somewhat standard food delivery app that is fairly similar to other well-known food delivery apps, however, the unique feature that differentiates itself from the others is that it has a small robot that delivers food to customers that place an order from a restaurant nearby of the four-kilometre radius. 

 

 

Motivation

 

The reason we came up with this project idea is that food delivery apps such as UberEATS, Menulog, Deliveroo, and DoorDash are becoming more and more popular, as people may be too busy or are too tired to cook but are hungry and want something to eat. Therefore, they would usually choose to order food from the apps mentioned. Furthermore, due to the current global pandemic food delivery apps have become a necessity to some households as some of them may not have any food left at home or they may also be craving a late-night meal such as a Halal snack pack which would currently only be possible through the use of a food delivery app as a result of the curfew that is put in place. We as a group thought it would be a great idea to come up with a project that can potentially make improvements to the current food delivery trend and have its unique features.

 

 

Description

 

This is a food delivery app that has a robot delivery feature that makes it unique from all the other food delivery apps. When the user first downloads the app it will require him or her to sign up an account, then set up their delivery address, it will then notify them of the restaurants that are in the four-kilometre radius that will be using a robot to deliver the food to the designated location. 

 

However, customers will still have the option to choose between a delivery person or a robot to deliver their food to them. All the robots will have many features such as a GPS and a live tracker to navigate the robot to the correct destination and allows the user to have a live tracking feature with estimated arrival time on the mobile app. It also has numerous cameras embedded around the entire robot giving it a 360-degree view and a sensor that detects anything that is getting close in contact so that it can avoid a collision, navigate around obstacles and can climb up a staircase if necessary, to reach its destination. As for security measures, there is a GPS installed in the robot so they can always be tracked and will also have a loud alarm system installed that triggers, when the robot has been picked up by someone and the robot, will automatically start recording to capture the thief’s appearance, the recording from the robot will be stored in a secure folder on a computer and backed up several times for future use if necessary. Furthermore, the only way to open the robot and retrieves whatever is inside it is to access it via the mobile app where there will be a 4 digit code that the customer receives after placing their order and will be required to input on the delivery robots touch screen to open it. Although the robot will be small in terms of size, but can still carry up to 10 kilograms of food at a speed of around 5 mph.

 

This project aims to build a more reliable and cheaper alternative for both consumers and businesses where consumers don’t have to worry about whether or not their food is being eaten by a stranger which also drastically improves the safety of the customer's food and businesses can save money at the same time while improving customer satisfaction.

 

 

Tools and Technologies

 

Some technologies and tools that will be required to complete this project, would be a reliable service where you can host the mobile app on. Along with a high-end computer system and a strong internet connection to ensure that the application will stay up and running with no hosting issues.

 

 

Skills required

 

For this project, I will need to find the appropriate food delivery app development partners who ideally have experience in developing a food delivery app to ensure that they have an understanding of how to build the structure of the app and potentially ways to improve it from previous experience to help build the app. I will also need to find a reliable manufacturer that has a good reputation and have set a fair price to make these unique delivery robots. The purpose of this is to build a long-lasting business relationship with the manufacturer for the future, in the case that this app was to expand to other areas more robots will be needed. Although the demand for certain IT jobs has changed, our ideal jobs are still the same because we are all passionate about the ideal job that we chose individually.

 

 

Outcome

 

Once the mobile delivery app has been successfully built all orders made on the app that are near the restaurant will be delivered using a delivery robot. Overall, this will help the business save a lot of money in the last-mile delivery costs and can potentially increase the use of online food delivery services which in turn leads to an increase in profits.
